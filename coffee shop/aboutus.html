Welcome to Kahawa

Brewing passion, one cup at a time.

Our Mission

At Kahawa, we believe that coffee is more than just a drink—it’s a way of life. We’re here to offer you an experience that awakens your senses and ignites your passion for premium, hand-crafted coffee.

From our sustainably sourced beans to our artisanal brewing methods, every cup at Kahawa is a blend of passion, precision, and love for the craft.

Our Vision

Our vision is simple—to become a community hub where coffee lovers can gather, connect, and savor the rich flavors of our locally roasted beans. At Kahawa, every cup is an invitation to slow down, appreciate life’s little moments, and feel part of something bigger.

We aim to promote ethical sourcing and sustainable practices, ensuring every bean we brew has a positive impact, from farm to cup.

Our Story

It all began with a love for coffee and a desire to create something special. Founded in 2024, Kahawa is the culmination of years of exploration, learning, and perfecting the art of coffee brewing. We started as a small coffee cart in the heart of the city, and thanks to our loyal customers, we’ve grown into the vibrant coffee shop we are today.

Our founder, Horace Njoroge and Collins ndung'u, envisioned a place where quality coffee meets a welcoming atmosphere, where people could gather and enjoy the richness of African coffee culture. Today, Kahawa is proud to serve ethically sourced beans from Kenya, Ethiopia, and beyond, brewed to perfection by our skilled baristas.
